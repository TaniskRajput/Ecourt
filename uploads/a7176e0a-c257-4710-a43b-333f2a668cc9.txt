People call me jack of all trades, Because i have always been a person with so many interests and music is one of them.
I listen to music of every genre and moreover i love to sing. My family has a history of producing good musicians, I have grown up listening to the sound of tablas and harmonium.
My grandfather however, was very famous in our locality due to his tremendous talent in music.
I still remember the time when i used to sit beside my grandfather while he shows the art of his hands on the tabla. Personally, I love to sing ,to participate in musical events, to showcase everyone that i am carrying what my ancestors have left.
I do not see music only as a form of entertainment but something which motivates me, some thing which brings the child out of me.
